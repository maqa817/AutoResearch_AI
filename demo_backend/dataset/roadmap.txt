Future Product Roadmap (2025-2026)
=================================

Phase 1: Foundation (Current)
- Implementation of core Multi-Agent orchestration.
- Support for local PDF and Web-based research.
- Real-time research progress dashboard.

Phase 2: Enterprise Integration (Q3 2025)
- Native integration with Slack, Microsoft Teams, and Notion for automatic report sharing.
- Support for multi-modal research (Analyzing images, charts, and diagrams in PDFs).
- Custom fine-tuning module for private company documentation.

Phase 3: Autonomous Operations (2026)
- Scheduled research "Watchdogs" that monitor the web 24/7 for specific trend changes.
- Integration with CRM systems (Salesforce/HubSpot) to automatically enrich lead data.
- Collaborative research sessions (Multiple humans working with multiple AI agents).
