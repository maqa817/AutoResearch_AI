Specific Capabilities of AutoResearch AI
====================================

The system excels at the following specialized tasks:

1. Competitive Intelligence: Automatically tracking competitor price changes and product launches.
2. Technical Summarization: Turning complex 50-page PDF research papers into 1-page summaries.
3. Sentiment Tracking: Monitoring social media and news for changes in brand perception.

Supported Connectors:
- Web: Tavily, DuckDuckGo, Google Search.
- Documents: PDF, Markdown, JSON, CSV.
- Export: PDF reports, Slack notifications, Webhooks.
