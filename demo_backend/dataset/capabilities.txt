Capabilities and Use Cases
==========================

AutoResearch AI can be used for a wide range of research tasks:

1. **Market Analysis**: Gathering data on competitors, trends, and market sentiment.
2. **Academic Research**: Finding and summarizing research papers on specific topics.
3. **Internal Documentation Search**: Indexing and searching through large volumes of company PDFs.
4. **Report Generation**: Automatically creating structured reports from raw data.

The system supports:
- PDF, TXT, and Markdown files.
- Web-augmented research via DuckDuckGo and Tavily.
- Multi-step reasoning and self-correction.
- Exporting results to various formats.
